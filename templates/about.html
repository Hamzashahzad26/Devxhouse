Our working strategy

We look at your requirements from analysis to development

The planning will be specifically crafted to meet your project, so you can be confident of that. We use the widely accepted Agile methodology. Although we adore working with Scrum, our project managers are adaptable and can determine the best course to take regardless of the methodology employed.

First encounter
  • We examine the materials you sent us and put together a team to analyze the project.
  • We also respond to your initial inquiries and let you know what the project's anticipated cost would be.
  • We want to understand your business objectives, your product vision, and the key features during our initial conversation.
    • Establishing a backlog
  • The time for more in-depth sessions will come once we have a rough idea of what needs to be done.
  • These sessions will enable us to define the requirements in the form of a product backlog and provide us with more precise estimates.
  • Additionally, we define the project's MVP (minimum viable product), which will enable phased implementation and gradual application development.
    • Sprint
  • We agree with you on the project's workflow and management strategy.
  • The backlog of tasks must have their implementation and sequence specified.
  • Now is the time to get prepared to begin working on your project. We spend some time setting up the environment and configuring the necessary tools.
    • Development
  • We remain in touch with you while adjusting flexibly to any changes.
  • This enables regular progress checks and ongoing process optimization for project management.
  • Depending on the management model used, the development phase progresses differently. We typically aim for Scrum's iterative work style.
    • Release
  • We are prepared to release the first version once the project's intended scope has been completed.
  • It is possible to repeat the iteration phase that ended with the release of a version multiple times.
  • More than one version of a product is typically offered when it is being created. Everything is based on the strategies put in place.
    • Project support
  • Normally, after a program is released, it needs to be maintained and its security is taken care of.
  • The complexity of our services guarantees that you won't be left holding the bag once the project is done.
  • We provide assistance for your project, which includes adding new updates, and new functionalities, and fixing mistakes as necessary.
